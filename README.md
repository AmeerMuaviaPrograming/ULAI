🏫 University of Layyah AI Chatbot (ULAI)

🥈 2nd Position Winner – South Punjab Hackathon 2026

An AI-powered chatbot developed for the University of Layyah to assist visitors, prospective students, and current students by providing quick and accurate information through a modern web interface.

🏆 Achievement

This project was presented at the South Punjab Hackathon 2026 on 15 June 2026, where our team secured 2nd Position.

🎯 Problem Statement

Students and visitors often struggle to find important university information quickly. This chatbot was designed to provide instant responses related to admissions, campus information, and student life.

✨ Key Features
🎓 Admissions and program information
💰 Eligibility criteria and fee structure guidance
🏛️ Campus and department information
👨‍🏫 Faculty directories and desk extensions
📚 Exam guidelines and university rules
🎉 Campus events and student life information
🤖 AI-powered conversational assistance
💻 Fast and user-friendly web interface
